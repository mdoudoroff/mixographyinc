JIM MEEHAN

Jim Meehan, product designer, journalist, and author of The PDT Cocktail Book, has worked in nearly every capacity of the hospitality business for the latter half his of his 37 years on the planet.

A Chicago area native schooled in Wisconsin, who achieved acclaim for his work behind bars in New York City; he now spends almost as much time on the road at trade shows and trainings as at home in New York City overseeing his world famous bar.

Jim’s passion for bartending, quest for further knowledge, belief in the transformative power of hospitality, and commitment to mentor the service industries next generation is only divided by his duties as a proud father, devoted husband, and doting French Bulldog companion.
